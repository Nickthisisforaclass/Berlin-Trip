Why Berlin?

Berlin is a city with a unique history, famous landmarks, and a vibrant culture. It’s known for its diverse neighborhoods, creative scene, and dynamic energy. Whether you’re interested in history, music, technology, or food, Berlin offers something for everyone.

Top Sights in Berlin

  • Brandenburg Gate: An iconic symbol of Berlin and German unity.
  • Berlin Wall Memorial: Explore the history and impact of the Berlin Wall.
  • Museum Island: A collection of world-class museums on a scenic island.
  • Alexanderplatz: A central square with shopping, dining, and the TV Tower.

Travel Tips

  • Consider getting a Berlin WelcomeCard for discounted public transport and attractions.
  • Many locals speak English, but learning a few German phrases can be helpful.
  • Berlin is very walkable and has excellent bike paths.
  • Don’t miss out on local foods like currywurst and döner kebab.
